## confirm

```js

```

<<<<<<< HEAD
`confirm` 函数显示一个带有 `question` 和两个按钮的模态窗口：确定和取消。
=======
The function `confirm` shows a modal window with a `question` and two buttons: OK and CANCEL.
>>>>>>> 30f1dc4e4ed9e93b891abd73f27da0a47c5bf613
